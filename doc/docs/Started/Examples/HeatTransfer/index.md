
# Heat transfer 

Verification of the Heat Transfer equation is made with the following tests:

- 1D Transient temperature distribution in a semi-infinite solid with constant surface heat flux (see [Example 1](example1/index.md))
- 1D Transient temperature distribution in a semi-infinite solid with surface convection (see [Example 2](example2/index.md))
- 2D Stationnary temperature distribution in a solid with surface convection and radiation (see [Example 3](example3/index.md))
